How might I get the most out of fatigued periods

  1. Rest if that can help the fatigue, e.g.:
    1. Clear mental processing stack
      1. Sleep
      2. Spend time thinking
      3. Mind-wandering
        1. Take a walk
      4. Relax
      5. Mindfulness
    2. Physical rest
  2. Use the fatigued time for menial, routine tasks
