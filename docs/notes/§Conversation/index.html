::Sync conflict! External update: 2021-06-27 at 13:16:: Click here to see original Bear note Conversations are like a dance; they are improvisational, you need to dance /with/ your partner to get a good conversation.

What makes someone a good conversationalist? Part of that is being honest, open-minded, kind, humble and creative. To allow room to express thoughts and emotions, and to care about doing that with someone (Part of enjoying conversations is feeling the emotions of the other).

New-born ideas are fragile Newborn ideas are a challenge to your imagination, not your critical thinking
