What am I looking for in a PhD

My ideal PhD:

The problem * MIP helps make the world a better place * MIP comes with DALY improvement or MIP is novelty search A proxy for how useful it is: * MIP is neglected

For personal career: * MIP allows me to learn useful things * MIP allows transferable skills * MIP signals my competence when completed * MIP is in a good environment for my career

And for thriving: * MIP is interesting * MIP is something I’d be good at
