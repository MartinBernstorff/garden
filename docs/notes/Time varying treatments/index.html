Time varying treatments

When there is treatment-confounder feedback, conventional methods for confounding adjustment do not work.

Kan “løses” med g-metoder: * G-formula * G-estimation * Inverse probability waiting

Q. A study has treatment-confounder feedback. What methods do you need to correct for it? A. G-methods

Q. Name an example of treatment-confounder feedback A. Hemoglobin 1 -> Epo -> Hemoglobin 2
